# BPDM Cleaning Service Dummy

The BPDM Cleaning Service Dummy will offers provider to figure out how to connect the BPDM Pool and the Gate applications to form the golden record process: The
process on how a record in the Gate becomes a golden record in the Pool which, in turn, gets distributed back to all Gates.
